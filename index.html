I love science, especially chemistry. I have a BS in chemistry from Rice University. While at Rice, I researched solar cells and published a first author paper. I then earned a MS in chemistry from the University of Chicago, where I was a teaching assistant for organic chemistry. I currently teach high school chemistry (regular, honors, and AP) in South Florida.

I have tutored students for the past 5 years in high school chemistry (regular, honors, and AP), SAT prep, and college chemistry (general chemistry and organic chemistry). I love teaching students to understand chemistry, rather than just memorize the facts. By learning to reason through science problems, chemistry becomes both easier and more interesting!

I tutor high school chemistry students on Sunday afternoons at Panera Breads in Aventura (on Biscayne Blvd. near Fresh Market). Please check above for my availability outside this time. I tutor one-on-one, or in pairs if you know another chemistry student that would like tutoring at the same time.
